About Me

Hello! I'm Joseph Perez, and I wear a few different hats depending on what the project needs.

My background spans technical writing, front-end web development, and business consulting. I love helping people clarify their ideas, build practical digital tools, and navigate the ever-changing landscape of technology and AI.

Whether I'm writing documentation that actually makes sense, prototyping a simple web app, or advising small businesses on AI strategy, my goal is always the same: make complex things accessible and actionable.

When I'm not working, you'll find me exploring Seattle's coffee scene, tinkering with new web tools, or reading about the intersection of technology and creativity.
